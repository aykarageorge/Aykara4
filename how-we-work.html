How We Work

Our Process

A simple, predictable process

Step 01

Tell us your idea

Every project starts with a conversation. Book a free 30-minute discovery call — no forms, no questionnaires, just a direct conversation about what you need.

We ask the right questions: What problem are you solving? Who will use it? What does success look like for you? And we are honest — if we think there is a better approach or a simpler solution, we will tell you.

Step 02

We scope and quote — in 48 hours

After our call, we put together a clear proposal. Not a vague estimate — a specific document covering what we will build, what is out of scope, how long it will take, and exactly what it will cost.

No jargon. No hidden fees. No "it depends." If something is genuinely uncertain, we will tell you that too — and give you options.

Step 03

We build it — and keep you in the loop

Once you approve the proposal, we start building immediately. You get weekly progress updates and access to a staging environment where you can see and test what we have built.

We use AI-assisted development tools that let us move faster than traditional teams. Every week you see real progress — not status reports and meetings.

04
Step 04

You go live — and we stay close

Before launch, we run thorough testing across devices and scenarios. We handle the deployment to your hosting environment and provide a handover session so your team knows how to use what we have built.

After launch, we are available for bug fixes, updates, and your next feature. Most clients stay on a monthly retainer because they keep building — and we keep delivering.

FAQ

Common questions

How long does a typical project take?

Most projects take 4–8 weeks from kickoff to launch. Smaller tools and MVPs can be done in 2–3 weeks. Larger platforms with many features may take longer — we will tell you upfront and give you milestones so you can see progress every week.

What if I don't know exactly what I need?

That is what the discovery call is for. Many of our best projects started with a client who had a clear business problem but no idea what the solution looked like technically. We help you scope it.

Do you work with clients outside Kerala?

Yes. We work remotely with clients across India and internationally. All project communication happens via video call, Slack, and our shared project management tools — timezone-friendly.

Who owns the code?

You do. Once the project is paid for, all source code, designs, and documentation belong to you. We hand over everything — no lock-in.

What happens after launch?

We offer a 30-day post-launch support period included in every project. After that, most clients move to a monthly retainer for ongoing development, maintenance, and support. There is no obligation — you can take the code and manage it yourself if you prefer.

Ready to start?

The first step is a 30-minute call. No commitment, no cost — just a conversation about what you need and how we can help.
