Why FDE Works Differently

FDE vs Traditional Agency vs In-House

Traditional Agency

Problem: You describe → they build → they hand off → you get stuck maintaining. Speed feels fast at first, then breaks down. You're not their problem once the contract ends.

Forward Deployed

Our Way: We embed. We ship. We iterate alongside you. We see every deploy. We fix what breaks. Your win is our win — aligned, fast, and unsupervised.

In-House Team

The Cost: Hiring, hiring managers, office, benefits, ramp-up time. You're building headcount and long-term burn rate. Gets expensive fast.

Is FDE Right for You?

Perfect if you are...

A Founder or CEO

You need software shipped fast without building an engineering team. You want to stay in the loop and iterate based on user feedback — not wait months for a "final" version.
